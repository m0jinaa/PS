### 문제 설명

<p>Bob Roberts likes to listen to music while he drives, but the car he owns is a little antiquated. No Bluetooth or USB connections here, but at least he has a CD player, so he's been transferring a lot of his music to CDs. At the moment he has only two CDs left and would like to get as much of his remaining music as possible on them. Given the capacity of the CDs and collection of songs, can you help him find the maximum number of minutes of music he can put on the two CDs?</p>
