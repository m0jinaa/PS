### 문제 설명

<p>A Paladin, a warrior adept at holy magic, needs your help forming spells. Being a Paladin, every spell must also be a <em>Palindrome</em>, meaning that it is the same string when read forwards and backwards. The cost of constructing a new spell is based on rune pair costs (costs of adjacent letters) that occur in the final spell. Rune pairs not presented in the input are not allowed in the final spell.</p>

<p>The total cost of a spell is the sum of all rune pair costs in the spell for each time the pair occurs in the spell. For example, if the spell is <strong><code>abacaba</code></strong>, then the cost is the sum of the costs of <strong><code>ab</code></strong> + <strong><code>ba</code></strong> + <strong><code>ac</code></strong> + <strong><code>ca</code></strong> + <strong><code>ab</code></strong> + <strong><code>ba</code></strong>.</p>

<p>Determine the smallest cost to make a new Paladin spell of exactly a given length.</p>
